# JQ AI Skills v0.6.8

JQ AI Skills v0.6.8 adds a first-skill candidate pack.

The candidate pack gives visitors a ready fake README, release note, and workflow summary for the first `github-safe-publisher` review. It closes the gap after the first skill walkthrough: instead of inventing sample files, visitors can run the safe review workflow against fictional public-ready material.

- First-skill candidate pack: https://github.com/jqaisystems/jqai-ai-skills/blob/main/docs/examples/first-skill-candidate/README.md
- First skill walkthrough: https://github.com/jqaisystems/jqai-ai-skills/blob/main/docs/examples/first-skill-walkthrough.md

Safety note: the candidate pack is fictional and public-safe. Do not replace it with credentials, account data, logs, exports, customer records, screenshots, unpublished prompts, private project notes, private source, or local private paths.
