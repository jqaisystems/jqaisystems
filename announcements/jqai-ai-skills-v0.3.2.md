# JQ AI Skills v0.3.2 Is Live

Public-safe announcement copy for the JQ AI Skills v0.3.2 release and sample research brief.

## LinkedIn

JQ AI Skills v0.3.2 is live.

This is a small release, but an important one: the public skills library now includes a fake-data sample brief for `research-brief-curator`.

The sample shows how public source records can become a short Markdown brief with public-ready highlights, needs-review items, archive decisions, and manual checks before anything gets shared.

That is the pattern I care about: useful AI workflows should not live only as prompts. They should become reviewable folders, examples, scripts, and safety gates that someone else can inspect before using.
