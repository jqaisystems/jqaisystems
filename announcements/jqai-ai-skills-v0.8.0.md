# JQ AI Skills v0.8.0

JQ AI Skills v0.8.0 adds nine new skills, growing the public library from 12 to 21. It is the largest release so far.

The new skills cover the freelancer's working week: PHP form mailing and branded client intake questionnaires that work as a pair, cold outreach drafting and lead scoring for review-first prospecting, brand voice linting, Obsidian daily notes and idea capture, llms.txt generation for AI search visibility, and a report-first folder cleanup janitor. All nine use ask-once configuration files instead of hardcoded personal data, and draft or report before they act.

No personal data, credentials, client material, pricing, or private prompts ship inside the skill files, and the repo-level public safety scan reports READY.
