# JQ AI Skills v0.6.2

JQ AI Skills v0.6.2 adds a visitor paths page for the public skill library.

The new page gives visitors three clear routes:

- evaluate the library;
- install one skill;
- publish public proof safely.

It is designed as a compact routing layer for people who want the right next document without reading the full repo first.
